Interactive Kiosk

Opportunity

To build an interactive kiosk that would welcome visitors to a newly renovated cathedral, and thank the generous donors who made that renovation and restoration possible. As part of the future and ongoing development, the kiosk served as a secondary purpose of informing visitors about cathedral and bishop history.

Research & Testing

The team evaluated Drupal and what was required to provide working templates and styling to the client, and downloaded and tested locally. We also ordered in equipment to confirm all would support and hook up as specked (definitely don't want any surprises on installation day).

My Role

  • Partnered with the Exhibits & Displays team and client for requirements gathering, provided detailed hours and cost for the interactive kiosk portion.
  • Produced sitemap, wireframe, and mockup.
  • Managed the final development of the touch screen kiosk, including UX/UI review and testing, and handing off final interactive files, style guide and Drupal templates to the client.

Outcome

We did a deep dive into the steps required to accomplish early on, and it paid off. This project was accomplished below budget and the client was extremely pleased with the end product that was installed and ready to go for the grand reopening of the cathedral.
