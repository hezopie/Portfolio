User Behavior & Testing:

Get in front of real users early

We try to get inside the head of users, but we all process information differently. The importance of never making assumptions and getting real people in front of our product (at the wireframe stage) is worth the extra time and effort. As an avid internet browser, what may seem obvious to me is not necessarily intuitive for everyone.

I used the form below to gather information from a group of testers engaging with our intranet interactive wireframe early on in the project.

Testing users for me ensures that the product served up offers the best kind of experience... one that engages and triggers some sort of emotion, be it excitement or curiosity or something entirely different. The experience can help guide the user quickly and effortlessly to get where they want to go (but really it's about where you want them to go).
