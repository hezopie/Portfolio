Medical Education Product

Opportunity

For the SASS medical education product, I supported solutions for graduate and undergraduate medical students. The graduate program was well-established, and the undergraduate program was new. Both evolved primarily from a pre-UX team. My work varied from producing wireframes for small feature updates to research, testing, wireframes, and mocks for full enhancements.

Research & Testing

Research and planning included qualitative interviews with students or residents at medical learning universities to get feedback on feature enhancements early on. Sessions included small groups of participants where a series of scripted user research questions guided us in gaining reactions and honest feedback for users of the product. We produced a readout summarizing research findings, key themes, and next steps and presented that to the team and key stakeholders. I created prototypes for developer review, iterated, and presented again for feasibility. If we saw the need, we also provided an interactive prototype for customer focus group sessions for additional validation, and another round of summarized results readout.

My Role

  • Oversee user testing sessions including invites, scheduling, interviews and follow-up emails .
  • Develop user testing results readout.
  • Produce wireframes, mocks and any digital component library updates.
  • Collaboration and iterations with product and development .

Outcome

Lean UX and mocks, paired with written Jira stories for a more efficient development turnaround.
