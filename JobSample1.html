Medical Education Product

Opportunity

For the SASS medical education product, I supported solutions for graduate and undergraduate medical students. The graduate program was well-established, and the undergraduate program was fairly new. Both evolved primarily from a pre-UX team. My work varied from producing wireframes for small feature updates to research, testing, wireframes and mocks for full enhancements.

Research & Testing

Research and planning included qualitive interviews with students or residents at medical learning universities to get feedback on feature enhancements and get an early view of product vision & help shape priorities. Sessions included small groups of participants where a series of scripted user research questions were asked to help gain reactions and honest feedback A readout was produced which summarized research findings, key themes and next steps and was presented to team and key stakeholders to finalize a plan of action I produced a prototype for developer review, iterated and presented again for feasibility. If we saw the need, we also produced an interactive prototype for customer focus group sessions to get additional validation, and another round of summarizing results and producing a readout

My Role

  • Oversee user testing sessions including invites, scheduling, interviews and follow-up emails
  • Results readout
  • Wireframes, mocks and any digital component library updates
  • Collaboration and iterations with product and development

Outcome

Lean UX and mocks paired with written Jira stories for more efficient development turnaround.
