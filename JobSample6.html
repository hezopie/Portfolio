High School Graduation

As my only child graduated, I wanted to do something creative and digital (because, you know… I like that sorta thing). I also knew many of my non-technical relatives and friends would respond better to a traditional printed invite (one that they could throw on the fridge and not forget about). I created this invite and emailed invitations with a downloadable version, and followed up with a printed version sent by mail.

And of course, I carried out the same theme for the open house decor including a banner and yard signs. Looking back… the day went by way too fast. And now… what seems like just a few blinks of the eye, he’s now almost ready to graduate college.
