Wireframe

Prototype the full experience and identify usability issues early on.

A prototype of all pages, with full flow and interactivity is essential. I like the wireframe to include every page and show every click. Having extensive insight to the experience of users that may interact with the product is an amazing process to walk through.

The wireframe helps my team and the client understand the end product earlier, resulting in less occurance of major changes later. Being able to provide a visual understanding before the creative phase gets under way is much easier to adapt than a concept design. It also serves as a clear guide for a functionality plan, setting milestones, and producing timelines.
