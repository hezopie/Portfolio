Understand/Empathize

We try to get inside the head of users, but we all process information differently. The importance of never making assumptions and getting real people in front of our product is worth the extra time and effort. As an avid internet browser, what may seem obvious to me is not necessarily intuitive for everyone.

Research/Define (challenge your assumptions)

Interviews

Focus Groups

Surveys

Usability testing

Discover… learn as much about… everything, before anything begins. Once the info is gathered, I organize and document steps and tasks, group by importance, level of effort, the end goal, role, and progress. Next, it’s time to work out the best combination of steps that will achieve the outcome we need that aligns with the mission and goals, and assign the appropriate team member(s) to follow-up and/or brainstorm.
