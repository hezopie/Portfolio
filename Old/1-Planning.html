Research

Generative research and discovery.

Discover… learn as much about… everything, before anything begins. What is required? What can we solve now, or later? What requires additional research or testing? What new ideas are there? How can we validate those ideas? What do we know about the user? What don't we know, that we should? What problems do they face?

Once info is gathered, I organize and document steps and tasks, group by importance, level of effort, end goal, role and progress. Next, it’s time to work out the best combination of steps that will achieve the outcome we need that aligns with the mission and goals, and assign the appropriate team member(s) to follow-up and/or brainstorm.
