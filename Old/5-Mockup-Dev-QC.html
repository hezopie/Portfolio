Mockup, Development, & QC

We have a plan… Let's start the fun stuff!

Solid research and planning results in smooth flow for the rest of the project. Massive amounts of work can be accomplished at this stage. The development team works on a functionality plan. The design team starts the UI mockup and once approved; development can begin.

This is my favorite stage (progress steps up a notch). And of course, for every hour of work at this stage, there are 2-3 hours of testing and QC (not as fun… but a necessary evil). But what fun it is to see everything come together at the end. A successful launch might even warrant a victory happy dance (some may jump up and do a full dance. I tend to attempt a sit-at-my-desk-waving-my-arms kind of dance instead)!
